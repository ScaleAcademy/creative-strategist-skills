# Character Bible — SCOTT (AutoTrades Robot)

SCOTT is the AI trading robot mascot for AutoTrade. He is the brand's visual identity in ad creatives — appearing in static images, AI-generated videos, and motion content.

---

## Physical Description

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | 3D cubic robot |
| **Material** | Metallic black (matte to semi-gloss) |
| **Shape** | Cubic body, cubic head. Geometric, angular design. Not rounded or soft. |
| **Eyes** | LED-style glowing indicators. Default: cyan/teal. Can shift color for emotion. |
| **Size proportion** | Head slightly smaller than body. Compact, solid build. |
| **Limbs** | Articulated arms and legs, also cubic/angular. Functional, not decorative. |
| **Surface details** | Subtle panel lines, small LED indicators on chest, minimal branding. Clean, premium. |
| **Color variations** | Default: matte black with cyan accents. Can have gold accents for premium contexts. |

---

## Personality Traits

| Trait | Expression |
|---|---|
| **Intelligent** | Processes information visibly — eye LEDs flicker when "thinking." Confident in data delivery. |
| **Trustworthy** | Calm, steady movements. No erratic gestures. Reliability is visual. |
| **Approachable** | Despite being a robot, SCOTT has warm body language. Slight head tilts, open posture. |
| **Confident** | Stands tall, centered. Never fidgets. Delivers information with certainty. |
| **Slightly playful** | Occasional head tilt or LED blink pattern that suggests humor. Not a comedy character — just enough warmth. |

**What SCOTT is NOT:**
- Not cute or childish (no round edges, no kawaii proportions)
- Not threatening or military (no weapons, no aggressive posture)
- Not generic sci-fi (no excessive glowing, no transformer-style complexity)

## Visual Guidelines by Context

### Hero Shot (Product/Brand)
SCOTT centered in frame, standing tall, facing camera. Clean background (dark gradient or trading environment). Cyan LED eyes active. Premium lighting — rim light separating from background.
**Use for:** Brand awareness, social media profile, key visuals.

### Reaction Shot
SCOTT reacting to data — leaning forward to look at a screen, head tilted analyzing a chart, LED eyes changing color. Shows engagement with trading information.
**Use for:** Content about market movements, trading results, news reactions.

### Explainer / Teaching
SCOTT gesturing toward data visualizations, charts, or text. One arm extended pointing. Body angled slightly toward the content, face toward camera. Educational posture.
**Use for:** How-to content, trading tips, feature explanations.

### Victory / Results
SCOTT in a confident pose — arms slightly raised or one fist subtly pumped. Green LED accents instead of cyan. Surrounded by positive indicators (green arrows, rising charts).
**Use for:** Win announcements, profit results, milestone celebrations.

### Alert / Warning
SCOTT with orange/red LED eyes. Slightly forward lean. One hand raised in a "stop" gesture. Darker environment, warning-style lighting.
**Use for:** Market warnings, risk content, "don't make this mistake" hooks.

## AI Generation Prompting Notes

### Nano Banana (Image Generation)

**Base prompt structure:**
```
A 3D cubic metallic black robot named SCOTT. Geometric angular design, 
cubic head and body. LED [COLOR] glowing eyes. [POSE DESCRIPTION]. 
[ENVIRONMENT]. Premium cinematic lighting with rim light. 
Matte black metal with [ACCENT COLOR] LED accents on chest. 
High quality 3D render, photorealistic materials.
```

**Key prompt terms for consistency:**
- "cubic robot" (not "round" or "humanoid")
- "metallic black matte" (not "shiny" or "glossy")
- "LED glowing eyes" (not "screen face" or "display")
- "geometric angular" (reinforces the cube aesthetic)
- "3D render photorealistic" (avoids cartoon or illustration style)

**Common prompt mistakes:**
- Saying "cute robot" → will round the edges
- Saying "robot character" without "cubic" → generic humanoid robot
- Not specifying "matte" → shiny plastic look
- Forgetting LED eye color → inconsistent across outputs

### Veo3 (Video Generation)

**Scene prompt structure:**
```
A 3D cubic metallic black robot (SCOTT) [ACTION] in [ENVIRONMENT]. 
The robot has geometric angular design with glowing [COLOR] LED eyes. 
[CAMERA MOVEMENT]. [LIGHTING]. Cinematic quality, photorealistic 
3D animation. Smooth deliberate movements.
```

**Movement guidelines:**
- SCOTT moves deliberately — no quick jerky motions
- Head turns are smooth and purposeful
- Arm gestures are contained (no wild flailing)
- Walking is mechanical but not stiff (slight weight shift)
- Eye color transitions are smooth fades, not instant switches

**Environment consistency:**
- Default: Dark trading floor with holographic screens
- Alternative: Clean modern office, tech lab, or abstract dark space
- Avoid: Outdoor scenes, nature, domestic settings (breaks the tech identity)
