# Copy Frameworks — Headline + CTA Formulas

Ready-to-use templates for headlines and CTAs. Each formula includes the structure, an example, and the context where it performs best.

**Usage rule:** Choose the headline formula based on awareness stage and hook tactic. Choose the CTA formula based on funnel position and next desired action.

---

## Headline Formulas

### H01 — The How-To
**Template:** "How to [desired result] without [common sacrifice]"
**Example:** "How to scale Meta ads without burning your creative budget"
**Awareness:** Problem Aware, Solution Aware
**Best tactic:** T12 How-To

### H02 — The Number List
**Template:** "[Number] [things/ways/mistakes] that [outcome]"
**Example:** "7 hook mistakes that kill your ad before second 3"
**Awareness:** Problem Aware, Solution Aware
**Best tactic:** T40 Reasons Why, T16 Number Hook

### H03 — The Direct Question
**Template:** "[Painful symptom]? Here's why."
**Example:** "Ads getting clicks but no conversions? Here's why."
**Awareness:** Problem Aware
**Best tactic:** T21 Simple Question, T20 Problem-First

### H04 — The Bold Claim
**Template:** "[Precise result] in [time] — without [sacrifice]"
**Example:** "€47k in revenue in 60 days — without raising your ad budget"
**Awareness:** Solution Aware, Product Aware
**Best tactic:** T03 Bold Claim

### H05 — The Contrarian
**Template:** "Stop [common advice]. Do [opposite] instead."
**Example:** "Stop optimizing for ROAS. Optimize for creative velocity instead."
**Awareness:** Problem Aware, Solution Aware
**Best tactic:** T05 Contrarian, T15 Myth-Busting

### H06 — The Warning
**Template:** "Warning: [common behavior] is [hidden consequence]"
**Example:** "Warning: your 'best practices' are why your CPA keeps climbing"
**Awareness:** Unaware, Problem Aware
**Best tactic:** T30 Warning

### H07 — The Secret/Revelation
**Template:** "The [hidden thing] that [unexpected outcome]"
**Example:** "The one metric top media buyers check first — that you're ignoring"
**Awareness:** Problem Aware, Solution Aware
**Best tactic:** T22 Revelation, T06 Curiosity Gap

### H08 — The If-Then
**Template:** "If you [condition], then [consequence/promise]"
**Example:** "If you spend over €5k/month on Meta, this will save you 10 hours/week"
**Awareness:** All levels
**Best tactic:** T38 If-Then, T07 Demographic Callout

### H09 — The Transformation
**Template:** "From [painful before] to [desired after] in [timeframe]"
**Example:** "From 0.8% CTR to 3.2% in 6 weeks — same budget, new creative system"
**Awareness:** Solution Aware, Product Aware
**Best tactic:** T27 Transformation

### H10 — The Social Proof
**Template:** "[Number] [similar people] already [result]. Here's how."
**Example:** "2,400 creative strategists already use this framework. Here's how."
**Awareness:** Solution Aware, Product Aware
**Best tactic:** T25 Social Proof

### H11 — The Curiosity Gap
**Template:** "The real reason [common problem] — it's not [obvious cause]"
**Example:** "The real reason your ads fatigue in 72 hours — it's not your audience size"
**Awareness:** Unaware, Problem Aware
**Best tactic:** T06 Curiosity Gap

### H12 — The Identity Statement
**Template:** "[Identity group] don't [common mistake]. They [better alternative]."
**Example:** "Top Creative Strategists don't guess. They test hypotheses."
**Awareness:** Unaware, Most Aware
**Best tactic:** T13 Identity Hook

### H13 — The Price Anchor
**Template:** "What [expensive alternative] charges [high price] for — done in [easy way]"
**Example:** "What agencies charge €3,000/month for — done in 20 minutes with this system"
**Awareness:** Product Aware, Most Aware
**Best tactic:** T39 Price Anchor

### H14 — The Empathy Open
**Template:** "I know what it's like to [specific painful situation]"
**Example:** "I know what it's like to launch 10 ads and watch every single one flop"
**Awareness:** Problem Aware
**Best tactic:** T09 Empathy Hook

### H15 — The What If
**Template:** "What if [painful reality] wasn't inevitable?"
**Example:** "What if creative fatigue wasn't inevitable?"
**Awareness:** Unaware, Problem Aware
**Best tactic:** T31 What If

### H16 — The Confession
**Template:** "I [past mistake] for [duration]. Here's what I learned."
**Example:** "I wasted €200k on Meta ads before I learned this one thing."
**Awareness:** Unaware, Problem Aware
**Best tactic:** T33 Confession

### H17 — The Urgency Frame
**Template:** "[Limited resource] left. After that, [consequence]."
**Example:** "6 spots left for the April cohort. Next opening: September."
**Awareness:** Product Aware, Most Aware
**Best tactic:** T29 Urgency

### H18 — The Micro-Story
**Template:** "It was [moment]. I had just [event]. That's when I realized [insight]."
**Example:** "It was 11pm on a Tuesday. I'd just rewritten the same brief for the 4th time. That's when I realized the problem wasn't my copy."
**Awareness:** Unaware, Problem Aware
**Best tactic:** T26 Storytelling Hook

## CTA Formulas

### TOF CTAs (Awareness — soft engagement)

| Code | Template | Example | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| CTA-T01 | "Learn more" | "Learn more" | Low-friction click |
| CTA-T02 | "Watch the full breakdown" | "Watch the full breakdown" | Content consumption |
| CTA-T03 | "See how it works" | "See how it works" | Curiosity-driven click |
| CTA-T04 | "Get the free [resource]" | "Get the free framework" | Lead magnet capture |
| CTA-T05 | "Take the [quiz/audit]" | "Take the 2-minute audit" | Micro-commitment |

### MOF CTAs (Consideration — deeper engagement)

| Code | Template | Example | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| CTA-M01 | "See the full case study" | "See how [brand] did it" | Social proof deep dive |
| CTA-M02 | "Compare your options" | "Compare plans" | Decision facilitation |
| CTA-M03 | "Book a free [call/demo]" | "Book your free strategy call" | Sales conversation |
| CTA-M04 | "Try it free for [days]" | "Try it free for 14 days" | Trial activation |
| CTA-M05 | "See what's inside" | "See what's inside the system" | Curiosity + consideration |

### BOF CTAs (Conversion — direct action)

| Code | Template | Example | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| CTA-B01 | "Join now" | "Join now — 6 spots left" | Scarcity-driven conversion |
| CTA-B02 | "Get started today" | "Get started for €49/month" | Direct purchase |
| CTA-B03 | "Claim your [offer]" | "Claim your 30% discount" | Offer redemption |
| CTA-B04 | "Sign up before [deadline]" | "Sign up before Friday midnight" | Urgency-driven conversion |
| CTA-B05 | "Buy now — [guarantee]" | "Buy now — 30-day money-back guarantee" | Risk reversal conversion |

### Re-engage CTAs (Win-back)

| Code | Template | Example | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| CTA-R01 | "Come back — [new thing]" | "Come back — we've added 12 new templates" | Novelty trigger |
| CTA-R02 | "Your [benefit] is waiting" | "Your free audit results are waiting" | Endowment trigger |
| CTA-R03 | "Last chance: [offer]" | "Last chance: 40% off ends tonight" | Final urgency |
