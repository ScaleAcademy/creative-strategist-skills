# Ad Formats & Specs — Cross-Platform Reference

| Platform | Format | Recommended ratio | Min resolution | Max size | Text overlay |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Meta** | Feed Image | 4:5 or 1:1 | 1080×1350 / 1080×1080 | 30 MB | < 20% of image |
| **Meta** | Stories / Reels | 9:16 | 1080×1920 | 30 MB | Avoid top/bottom 14% |
| **TikTok** | Spark Image | 9:16 | 1080×1920 | 5 MB | Native TikTok |
| **Snapchat** | Snap Ad | 9:16 | 1080×1920 | 5 MB | Avoid top/bottom 15% |
| **Pinterest** | Standard Pin | 2:3 | 1000×1500 | 10 MB | Light, integrated |
| **LinkedIn** | Sponsored Content | 1.91:1 or 1:1 | 1200×628 / 1200×1200 | 5 MB | Professional |
| **Google Display** | Responsive | 1.91:1 + 1:1 | 1200×628 + 1200×1200 | 5 MB | Format-dependent |
| **Google Display** | Fixed banner | Multiple | Format-dependent | 150 KB | — |

## Safe Zones

Areas to avoid for critical elements (text, logo, CTA):

| Platform | Area to avoid |
|---|---|
| **Meta Feed** | Top and bottom bands (Meta UI) |
| **Meta Stories / Reels** | Top 15% (progress bar) + bottom 25% (CTA / profile) |
| **TikTok** | Top 15% + bottom 35% (TikTok UI + CTA + profile + description) |
| **Snapchat** | Top 15% + bottom 20% (swipe-up button + interface) |
| **YouTube Reels** | Top 20% + bottom 30% |

**Universal rule:** Place all critical text (hook, title, CTA) between 15% and 65% of the total height on vertical 9:16 formats.

## Subtitles and Accessibility

| Platform | Subtitles | Recommended |
|---|---|---|
| Meta | Auto-generation available | Yes — 85% of views without sound |
| TikTok | Auto-captions available | Yes — native style |
| LinkedIn | Manual (SRT) recommended | Required — office audience |
| YouTube | Auto-generated by Google | Recommended |
| Snapchat | Manual or burn-in | Recommended |

**Subtitle format:** Burned into the video (burn-in) = more reliable cross-platform. SRT file = more flexible but depends on the player.

## Pre-Upload Checklist

For each creative:
- [ ] Ratio and resolution match the target platform
- [ ] Critical text in the safe zone
- [ ] Subtitles present (if video)
- [ ] File size under the limit
- [ ] Sound tested (with AND without)
- [ ] CTA visible and readable in < 2s
- [ ] Clear file name: `[brand]_[format]_[ratio]_[hook-id]_[date]`
