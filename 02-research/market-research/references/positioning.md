# Competitive Positioning Reference

## Positioning Axes (choose the most relevant pair)

| Axis A | Axis B | Use For |
|---|---|---|
| Rational | Emotional | Any product |
| Premium | Accessible | Price-sensitive categories |
| Expert | Peer | Trust-driven categories |
| Fast results | Sustainable | Health, fitness categories |
| General | Niche | Audience-specific products |

## White Space Logic
The best position is usually:
1. Where no strong competitor sits
2. Where the audience is underserved (check reviews for complaints)
3. Where your brand can credibly and consistently deliver

## Positioning Statement Template (internal use only)
> For [specific persona], [brand] is the [category] that [unique benefit] because [reason to believe].

This is a strategic anchor, not ad copy.
