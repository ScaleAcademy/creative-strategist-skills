# Content Consumption — Platforms & Register by Channel

Platform-specific content consumption patterns for ad creative planning. Each platform has its own audience expectations, content formats, and tone requirements.

**Usage rule:** Design creative for the platform first, then adapt to others. A TikTok ad repurposed for LinkedIn without adjustment will fail on both.

---

## Instagram

| Dimension | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Primary audience** | 18-44 (strongest: 25-34). Skews slightly female. Urban, lifestyle-oriented. |
| **Content that works** | Reels (short video 7-30s), Carousel (educational, storytelling), Stories (behind-the-scenes, polls). |
| **Tone/register** | Aspirational but authentic. Polished enough to fit the feed aesthetic, raw enough to feel real. |
| **Peak engagement** | Weekdays 11am-1pm, evenings 7-9pm. Sundays for long-form content. |
| **Ad format recommendations** | Reels ads (vertical video 9:16), Carousel ads (3-7 slides), Stories ads (full-screen vertical). |
| **Platform-specific rules** | Visual quality matters more than other platforms. Feed aesthetic is a filter. UGC must be "good-looking" UGC. Captions are important (many watch without sound). |
| **Avoid** | Landscape video, text-heavy images, overly corporate tone, content that breaks the feed aesthetic. |

---

## TikTok

| Dimension | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Primary audience** | 16-34 (growing older). Entertainment-first mindset. Meme-literate. |
| **Content that works** | Short video (7-30s), trend-based content, native-looking ads, creator-led, storytelling hooks. |
| **Tone/register** | Casual, authentic, fast-paced, slightly chaotic. The more it looks like organic content, the better it performs. |
| **Peak engagement** | Evenings 7-11pm, weekends. But TikTok's algorithm is less time-dependent than other platforms. |
| **Ad format recommendations** | In-Feed ads (vertical 9:16, 15-60s), Spark Ads (boost organic posts), TopView. |
| **Platform-specific rules** | Do NOT make ads. Make TikToks. Production polish = instant skip. Sound matters (trending audio). First 1 second is everything. Text overlay is essential. |
| **Avoid** | Polished production, corporate messaging, landscape format, slow intros, brand logos in the first 3 seconds. |

---

## YouTube

| Dimension | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Primary audience** | Broad (18-55+). Intent-driven: people search for content. More deliberate consumption. |
| **Content that works** | Long-form video (5-15 min for organic), pre-roll ads (6s bumpers, 15-30s skippable), Shorts (sub-60s). |
| **Tone/register** | Educational, informative, entertaining. Higher production quality expected than TikTok/Reels. Viewers accept longer content. |
| **Peak engagement** | Evenings and weekends. Lunch breaks for short content. |
| **Ad format recommendations** | Skippable in-stream (15-30s, hook in first 5s before skip), Bumper ads (6s non-skip), Shorts ads, Discovery ads. |
| **Platform-specific rules** | First 5 seconds are critical (before skip button). Audio is primary (most watch with sound). Thumbnail quality matters for Discovery ads. |
| **Avoid** | Starting with "Hey guys" or slow intros (5 seconds to skip), content that doesn't deliver on the thumbnail promise, poor audio quality. |

---

## Facebook

| Dimension | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Primary audience** | 30-65+. Skews older than Instagram. Mix of personal and interest-group usage. |
| **Content that works** | Video (15-60s), long-form text ads, link posts, carousel. Facebook users still read long copy. |
| **Tone/register** | Conversational but not too casual. Informative. Can be longer and more detailed than Instagram. |
| **Peak engagement** | Weekdays 9am-12pm, 1-3pm. Wednesday is historically strongest. |
| **Ad format recommendations** | Feed video (1:1 or 4:5), Carousel (for e-commerce), Link ads with strong primary text. Reels placement growing. |
| **Platform-specific rules** | Long primary text works (125+ characters). Social proof in comments drives performance. Video can be slightly longer. Retargeting is very effective on Facebook. |
| **Avoid** | Youth-oriented content, TikTok-style fast cuts for older demographics, ignoring the primary text (it gets read on Facebook). |

---

## LinkedIn

| Dimension | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Primary audience** | 25-55, professional context. Decision-makers, B2B buyers, career-focused. |
| **Content that works** | Thought leadership, case studies, industry insights, personal professional stories. Text posts with image, document carousels, video. |
| **Tone/register** | Professional but human. Expertise-led. First-person stories perform well. Avoid corporate jargon. |
| **Peak engagement** | Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am and 12-1pm. Business hours. |
| **Ad format recommendations** | Sponsored content (single image or video), Document ads (carousel PDFs), Message ads (InMail). |
| **Platform-specific rules** | B2B targeting is unmatched (job title, company size, industry). Content must provide professional value. Personal stories work if tied to professional insight. |
| **Avoid** | B2C tone, entertainment-first content, clickbait, overly sales-y messaging in the first touch. |

---

## Snapchat

| Dimension | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Primary audience** | 13-29. Younger than TikTok/Instagram. Communication-first platform. |
| **Content that works** | Full-screen vertical video (3-10s), AR filters, short punchy messages. |
| **Tone/register** | Playful, immediate, personal. Feels like messaging a friend, not watching content. |
| **Peak engagement** | Throughout the day (messaging behavior). Strongest in evenings. |
| **Ad format recommendations** | Snap Ads (3-10s vertical video), Story Ads, Collection Ads, AR Lenses. |
| **Platform-specific rules** | Ultra-short attention span. Message must land in 3 seconds. Sound on by default. Very young audience -- messaging must match. |
| **Avoid** | Long content, complex messaging, B2B tone, content designed for other platforms without adaptation. |

## Cross-Platform Summary

| Platform | Best length | Sound | Tone | Production | Primary CTA method |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Instagram | 7-30s | Off default | Aspirational-authentic | Medium | Swipe up / Link in bio |
| TikTok | 7-30s | On default | Native-casual | Low | In-video text + profile link |
| YouTube | 15-30s (ad) | On default | Educational-entertaining | Medium-high | Skip to site / End screen |
| Facebook | 15-60s | Off default | Conversational-detailed | Medium | Link click / Primary text |
| LinkedIn | 30-90s | Off default | Professional-human | Medium | Learn more / Message |
| Snapchat | 3-10s | On default | Playful-immediate | Low | Swipe up |
