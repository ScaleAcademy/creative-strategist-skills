# Brand Health Reference

## When to Use
When establishing a performance baseline before recommending new creative strategy.

## Go Here
Meta Ads Manager → Campaigns → set date range → export performance data.
Columns: Impressions, Reach, CPM, CPC, CTR, ThruPlay, Hook Rate, CPA/CPL, Spend, Results.

## Look For
- Hook Rate (ThruPlay/Impressions): industry avg 20–30% for Meta video
- CTR (link click): avg 1–3% for Meta feed; above 3% = strong creative
- CPL/CPA: varies by niche — establish baseline before judging
- Creative fatigue signals: frequency > 3 + CTR drop > 20% week-over-week

## Why This Matters
Without a baseline, you're optimizing blindly. The baseline reveals what's working (build on it), what's failing (kill it), and what's missing (fill the gap).

## Benchmark Reference (Meta Ads — General)

| Metric | Weak | Average | Strong |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hook Rate (video) | < 15% | 20–30% | > 35% |
| CTR (link) | < 0.8% | 1–2% | > 2.5% |
| CPM | > 20€ | 8–15€ | < 8€ |
| Frequency | > 4 | 2–3 | — |

*Benchmarks vary by niche and audience size. Always compare against your own historical data first.*
